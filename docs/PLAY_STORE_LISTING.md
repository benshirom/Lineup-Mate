## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**Lineup Mate** is the festival companion app built for real festival-goers.

### Build your personal schedule
Browse the full lineup, star the artists you don't want to miss, and get a clean day-by-day personal schedule. Conflict detection warns you when two artists overlap so you can plan ahead.

### Go together — group schedules
Create a group for your festival crew. Everyone marks their picks, and the shared view shows at a glance who's seeing what. Invite friends with a single code — they join in seconds.

### Works offline
Your schedule is always available, even without signal. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

### Always up to date
Lineup Mate uses silent over-the-air updates. The app refreshes in the background — no manual App Store update needed.

### Your data, your control
- Export a full copy of your data at any time (JSON)
- Delete your account instantly from the app
- We never sell your data

### Accessibility
Lineup Mate is built to WCAG 2.1 AA standards: full keyboard navigation, screen-reader labels, RTL (Hebrew) support, and reduced-motion respect.
